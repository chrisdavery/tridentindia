Our Story

ROOTED IN POINT REYES, CA

Connecting people to the raw beauty and transformative power of nature since 1991, Coyuchi elevated organic home goods to pioneer conscious luxury without compromise. Drawing from the hues and facets of nature, we create indulgently soft, heirloom-quality pieces that enliven your living spaces with effortless elegance.

Each element of a Coyuchi home flows with the pure restorative energy of the earth, sustainably sourced and thoughtfully crafted to inspire well-being and connect generations.
